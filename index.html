Why your telco
and your phone
disagree.

Network providers measure data at the tower. Your device measures it at the socket. The gap between these two measurement points ‚Äî protocol overhead, rounding, phantom charges ‚Äî is where your money disappears.

AI Data Meter's Discrepancy Algorithm continuously reconciles both data sources. When the variance exceeds 5%, we don't just alert you ‚Äî we build your case automatically.

  • 7-day personal baseline per app, not a global average
  • Anomaly trigger: >25% deviation from baseline during idle states
  • USSD and SMS parsing to capture telco-reported deductions
  • Time-window reconciliation to account for billing cycle offsets
  • Full forensic PDF generated with device ID, timestamps, and legal citations

NCC Compliance

Your legal rights,
automated.


Under the NCC General Consumer Code of Practice, Section 5.3, Nigerian consumers have the right to accurate billing and formal dispute resolution. Most people never exercise this right because the process is complex and time-consuming.

AI Data Meter's Dispute-Forge changes that. Every forensic audit report includes the legal citation, device evidence, and timestamped usage breakdown required for a valid NCC dispute ‚Äî generated in seconds, filed automatically.

AI Data Meter is actively working with the NCC to enable direct API submission of dispute reports, so your case goes straight to the right desk without you lifting a finger.
